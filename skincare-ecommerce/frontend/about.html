Our Story

Crafted with Purpose

We believe skincare should be simple, effective, and honest. That's Lumière.

How It Started

A Skin Problem That Changed Everything

Lumière was born in 2019 when founder Dr. Clara Chen, a biochemist with combination-sensitive skin, grew frustrated with products full of marketing promises but empty on results. She began formulating for herself, and the results were transformative.

Word spread. Friends wanted her formulas. Then strangers. By 2021, Lumière had launched officially — with a mission to bring dermatologist-grade skincare to everyone, without the confusion, without the toxins, and without the sky-high price tags.

Today, Lumière serves over 50,000 customers in 35 countries, all united by a desire for skincare that simply works.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every decision we make — from formulation to packaging — is guided by these principles.

🔬

Science First

Every ingredient is backed by clinical research. We don't add anything unless it has proven efficacy and a strong safety profile.

🌿

Clean Beauty

No parabens, phthalates, sulfates, artificial colors, or synthetic fragrances. Our "No List" has over 1,400 ingredients we'll never use.

🐰

Cruelty-Free

Certified by Leaping Bunny. We never test on animals and only partner with suppliers who share our commitment.

🌍

Sustainability

Recyclable packaging, carbon-neutral shipping, and a 1% for the Planet pledge. We're working toward a fully circular model by 2027.
